Basic Disclosure (Multiple Mode)

A disclosure component (also known as accordion or collapsible) allows users to show and hide content sections. This helps organize information and reduce cognitive load by allowing users to focus on one section at a time.

Simply click on any header to expand or collapse that section. In multiple mode, you can have several sections open at once.

The component is fully keyboard accessible and follows WCAG AAA guidelines.
  • Smooth expand/collapse animations
  • Single or multiple open modes
  • Keyboard accessible
  • ARIA compliant for screen readers
  • 44px minimum touch targets
  • Disabled state support
