Why: Faster to scan, works for long labels, mobile-friendly. Users complete forms 30% faster.

Application Types

Know What You're Building

Not all applications are the same. Standards adapt to context and user goals.

üìÑ

Brochure/Marketing

Goal: Convert visitors
  • Density: Low (airy, whitespace)
  • Spacing: 64-96px sections
  • Navigation: Simple top bar
  • Interaction: Scroll + Click
  • Examples: Landing pages, portfolios
üìä

App/Dashboard

Goal: Complete tasks efficiently
  • Density: High (dense, data-rich)
  • Spacing: 24-32px sections
  • Navigation: Persistent sidebar
  • Interaction: Keyboard + Click
  • Examples: Dashboards, SaaS apps
üì±

Social/Content

Goal: Engage users, time on site
  • Density: Medium
  • Spacing: 16-24px posts
  • Navigation: Sticky top bar
  • Interaction: Scroll + React
  • Examples: Social media, blogs, forums

Each type has different standards.

A marketing site needs big hero sections and CTAs. A dashboard needs dense data and keyboard shortcuts. Social needs infinite scroll and reactions. Use the RIGHT standards for your context.
